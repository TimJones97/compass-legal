Who are we?

We are an expanding general law practice based in Beenleigh, Queensland and our philosophy is simply to achieve the very best for our clients by providing practical, personalised legal advice and solutions.

Being committed, results-driven professionals, we display integrity, teamwork and open communication in everything we do.

Our Objectives

To carry out our mission, Compass Legal Solutions’ main objectives are to deliver excellent advice and assistance to our client which is always:

Legally accurate and up-to-date

Commercially applicable and appropriate to the clients’ circumstances

Provided in an innovative and pro-active manner

  • Develop with each and every client a strong long-lasting relationship
  • Ensure we have the best staff available and provide them with training and support
  • Use the latest technology possible
  • Manage the firm profitably to achieve continuing growth
